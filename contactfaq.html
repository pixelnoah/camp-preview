REGISTRATION/COST
How much does the Extended Care add-on cost?
The Extended Care (3:00 - 5:30 PM) optional add-on costs $25 per day or $100 per week (Monday - Friday). We'll send you the information to select your add-ons once you're signed up for CAMP/pixel! For more information about add-ons, please visit our Schedule page.
How much does the Daily Lunch add-on cost?
Stay tuned for more information about our Lunch add-on! For more information, please visit our Schedule page.
Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?
We know that summer camp costs aren't cheap, so we offer several ways to save on CAMP/pixel. These include early-bird, multi-week and sibling discounts, and low-APR financing options. Visit our Cost page for more details.
Why are some camp topics more expensive than others?
We've priced our offerings to accurately reflect the cost of the hardware, software, licenses, materials, and additional resources required to support each camp. Visit our Cost page for more information and ways to save!
I'm having trouble viewing the mobile version of your registration site. What's wrong?
For best viewing of our CAMP/pixel and ticketing websites, we recommend you use the Google Chrome browser on a laptop or desktop computer. Some webpages may not be optimized for mobile or tablet display.
How do I know if a camp is sold out?
On our Tickets page, sold-out camps are grayed out and not clickable in the schedule. You can see how many spots remain for each camp by looking right below the button for your desired camp. Reserve now to guarantee your spot!
I'm sold out of a camp! Is there a waiting list?
You can sign up for Availability Notifications on a sold-out camp via link directly below your desired camp on the Tickets page. We do recommend signing up for a different option within your week of choice to guarantee your spot at CAMP/pixel, but we'll notify the camp's active Availability Notifications list when a spot becomes available.
How can I add an add-on to my reservation?
Your CAMP/pixel registration comes with a personal account on our online portal. There, you can view, modify, and add to your camp experience. We email you all the details when you sign up!
What is your refund policy?
We offer 100% refunds on CAMP/pixel 2017 purchases for requests made before March 15, 2017. Please visit hq.pixelacademy.org/refunds to view our full policies on refunding or modifying a CAMP/pixel reservation.

It's up to you! CAMP/pixel is both a learning and a social experience; you're free to put your camper in whichever age group you think he or she would feel more comfortable.

Great question! Stay tuned for information on great opportunities for teens older than 14 to learn and lead with CAMP/pixel. Don't forget to subscribe to our mailing list to be the first to know our updates!

THE CAMP DAY
What are CAMP/pixel's hours?
CAMP/pixel runs from 9:00 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. Monday through Friday during 9 weeks of the summer. Flexible check-in is at 8:30-9:00 a.m. and flexible check-out is from 2:30-3:00 p.m. Parents wishing to pick up later can add on Extended Care, which runs until 5:30 p.m. Learn more on our Schedule page.
Can I see a sample schedule of a CAMP/pixel day?
Yes! Please visit our Schedule page to discover what a typical day looks like and call us at (866) 771-6837 if you have any questions.
Do campers just sit around and stare at the computer all day by themselves?
Absolutely not! In addition to on-screen learning, the camp day is packed with screen breaks, food breaks, physical activities, and more. Please visit our Schedule page to learn more.
Do you offer late pick-up?
Pick-up is at 2:30-3:00 p.m. You're welcome to add on Extended Care to your reservation, which gives you until 5:30 p.m. to pick up your camper. Learn more on our Schedule page.
Can we drop off early?
We invite you to drop off as early as 8:30 a.m. for flexible check-in. Drop-off before 8:30 is not allowed as we won't be staffed to support your camper at that time. Learn more on our Schedule page.
Can I stay with my child at CAMP/pixel?
We ask camper parents to leave once the check-in process is complete. We have every parent's contact information on hand and we don't hesitate to call; please do the same at (866) 771-6837 if you have any concerns!
How do I know my child is not just playing video games all day?
At CAMP/pixel, "free time" is earned and not abused. Campers may be found testing a game or mod they design or taking a few minutes for a well-earned fun break, but they know from day 1 that it's not what CAMP/pixel is all about. Learn more on our Schedule page.

WHAT TO BRING
What should my child bring to CAMP/pixel?
We have snacks and water here, but you're welcome to pack both if you prefer! If you don't sign up for our daily lunches, please pack a lunch along with your camper. We use our technology for learning, so no need to bring laptops or tablets. Most importantly, make sure your camper comes with a positive attitude and readiness to learn!
Should I pack a snack for my camper?
A twice-daily morning and afternoon snack are included in your reservation! You're also welcome to pack snacks of your own if you prefer.
Do I need to bring any technology?
Nope! All of our camps are taught using our technology. That said, if your camper would like to bring his or her own device in the event there's downtime in a day, he or she is welcome to do so.
Do I need a doctor-signed medical form to send my child to CAMP/pixel?
You do not! When you're signed up for camp and can access your Pixel Academy account, we'll ask you to please fill out your camper's Profile, which includes a box for Medical Information. We monitor and refer to this very closely and are happy to speak with any parents with medical or related concerns.
Should my camper bring a flash drive?
Every registered camper gets one free 8GB flash drive each summer! Campers take the drive home with their finished projects at the end of each week.
What do you do when it rains?
Water and technology: Not a great mix. Make sure your camper is dry at drop-off on rainy days (raincoats, boots)! We tend to avoid going outside on days with inclement weather.

MISCELLANEOUS
Is CAMP/pixel associated with Pixel Academy?
Yes! Pixel Academy runs CAMP/pixel during the summer and lots of other awesome, hands-on, future-focused technology learning during the year. If you like CAMP/pixel, join us during the year for after-school membership, a birthday party, gaming events, and much more!
Can adults participate?
Nope :( But take a look at our job opportunities if you're interested in joining the CAMP/pixel team!
Are you special needs friendly?
Our Instructors have experience and training with a variety of special needs and learning styles. CAMP/pixel creates an amazing environment for kids and teens who can think and act for themselves. In order to encourage creativity and facilitate a laid-back learning-environment, we try to keep our space an adult-free area (except for our Instructors); we unfortunately cannot currently accommodate anyone that requires an aide in school. If you have any special questions or concerns, please give us a call.
What if I have a special circumstance?
Please let us know as soon as you can so we can make every effort to adjust and prepare accordingly. We're happy to be flexible and do our best to find a solution to any scenario we can!
