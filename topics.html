CAMP/topics
Campers work on two projects throughout their CAMP/pixel experience, one PHYSICAL and one DIGITAL. Together, these projects form a powerful, fundamental look into a realm of technology campers may recognize later in college-level computer science and engineering classes. Each topic includes cutting-edge technology, expert instructors, 21st-century skills, a hands-on, inquiry-based learning experience unique to CAMP/pixel.
