CAMP/schedule
Every day at CAMP/pixel is packed from morning to afternoon with invigorating, inspiring activities both on and off the screen. Campers are surrounded by amazing tech resources and immersed in rewarding group activities and social experiences. We've created and time-tested a perfect blend of focus and fun to build a social, motivating experience in which imaginative young minds can thrive. For a part of each day, they'll be honed in on their with our expert instructors. For another, they'll be bonding with each other at the playground. They're with Pixel Academy staff throughout the day to ensure that they're always excited, engaged, and standing by our Rule #1: Have Fun.

LEARNING

DIGITAL PROJECT
The first half of our meticulously-planned Combos this summer is the Digital Project. The Telegraph says that "using technology in an educational environment not only better reflects children's life outside the classroom, but also allows them to hone their digital skills in a way that will continue to be valuable throughout their adult life." Whether it's coding or modding a video game, digital animation, or music production, this is your child's opportunity to imagine, plan, and use digital technology to create a project of their very own. Using industry-grade technology, campers complete a week-long journey with our instructors to understand and develop skills in new software, design a unique project, and leave with knowledge they'll see time and time again in and out of their education.
PHYSICAL PROJECT
As Scholastic put so well, "hands-on, creative, and artistic activities help students to focus and retain knowledge, and at the same time emphasize the importance of beauty and design in our world." Each topic's complement to a week's Digital Project is the Physical Project; while each camper spends a good amount of time producing something digital, he or she will work to produce a physical product as well. Campers can work on laser cutting, 3D design, DJing, and much more. Each Physical Project will require some on-screen and off-screen work to accomplish its goal.

DROP-OFF
AND PICK-UP
FLEXIBLE CHECK-IN
We're offering a flexible timeframe for your convenience, every day of CAMP/pixel. We won't start the activities until 9:15 a.m.; you're welcome to drop off as early as 8:30 a.m. for your camper to come and have a bit of free time before the camp day begins.
FLEXIBLE CHECK-OUT
Our program wraps up at 2:30 p.m. every day. You're welcome to drop in anytime between then and 3:00 p.m. to pick up your child. For security purposes, campers are only released from our supervision to individuals listed in their account. If you need time past 3:00 p.m., we offer Extended Care.
EXTENDED CARE* (3:00-5:30 p.m.)
Extended Care is a great option for campers who want more time to socialize and for parents who need to pick-up campers later than 3:00 p.m. After a full day of camp, Extended Care is not more of the same; instead of continuing to work with our Instructors and technology, campers in Extended Care have an opportunity to relax, socialize with one another, and play off-screen games.

SCREEN BREAKS
AND ACTIVITIES
CHALLENGE ACTIVITIES
Campers take several screen breaks throughout the Camp day as a chance to reset, move around, and socialize! Challenge Activities are kinetic, group-oriented games campers play for a few minutes to get to know each other and the instructors.
CIRCLE TIME
Every day begins and ends with a circle. It's an opportunity for the campers to ready, plan, reflect, and socialize to start and end the day. Your child will always have an opportunity to center him or herself before getting into the exciting activities of the day, and collect and reflect at the end. This is our first and last opportunity for us to ensure the entire group is following Rule 1: Have Fun.
OUTSIDE TIME
We love playing outside! Weather permitting, we head to a local playground around mid-day to take an extended screen break, get out some energy, and socialize and play together (yes, staff plays too!) On days when the weather prevents us from going outside, our inside activities promote the same goals.
FREE TIME/GAMING
Sitting around and passively consuming computer games isn't the CAMP/pixel formula. Depending on the topic, campers may earn a few moments to test the games they make or play on our servers as a well-deserved reward for hard work.

MEALS
HEALTHY SNACKS**
Two snacks per day are included in your CAMP/pixel ticket! We carefully select healthy, delicious snacks for our campers to nibble on during these social, 15-minute screen breaks. Clif bars, KIND Healthy Grains bars, and Annie's Fruit Snacks are examples from our past camps.
LUNCH TIME**
We set aside plenty of time for a mid-day reset for our campers. Check back soon for information on an optional add-on for fresh daily lunches, provided by CAMP/pixel!
** Please note that while we make our best efforts to make our food offerings healthy and available to all, we do not operate allergen-free facilities. Please send separate food items with your camper if it is a concern.
