LEARNING
DIGITAL PROJECT
The first half of our meticulously-planned Combos this summer is the Digital Project. The Telegraph says that "using technology in an educational environment not only better reflects children's life outside the classroom, but also allows them to hone their digital skills in a way that will continue to be valuable throughout their adult life." Whether it's coding or modding a video game, digital animation, or music production, this is your child's opportunity to imagine, plan, and use digital technology to create a project of their very own. Using industry-grade technology, campers complete a week-long journey with our instructors to understand and develop skills in new software, design a unique project, and leave with knowledge they'll see time and time again in and out of their education.
PHYSICAL PROJECT
As Scholastic put so well, "hands-on, creative, and artistic activities help students to focus and retain knowledge, and at the same time emphasize the importance of beauty and design in our world." Each topic's complement to a week's Digital Project is the Physical Project; while each camper spends a good amount of time producing something digital, he or she will work to produce a physical product as well. Campers can work on laser cutting, 3D design, DJing, and much more. Each Physical Project will require some on-screen and off-screen work to accomplish its goal.
