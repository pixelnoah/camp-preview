LASER CUTTING is the act of cutting super-precise vectors into various materials with an actual laser. At CAMP/pixel, campers can use vector-based design software (like Adobe Illustrator) to create their own unique designs that are cut on our totally safe equipment.

"Design is thinking made visual." -Saul Bass

Vector design is used to create digital images that can be made smaller or larger without losing resolution.
